Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how CarPartsCompare.co.uk uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide information to website owners, and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To understand how you use our website
  • To improve our services and content
  • To track affiliate referrals

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be switched off.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookieConsent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - maintains session state 2 years

Affiliate Cookies

These cookies track when you click through to a retailer, allowing us to earn commission if you make a purchase.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
aw* Awin affiliate tracking 30 days

4. Third-Party Cookies

When you click through to retailer websites, those sites may set their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies. Please refer to each retailer's cookie policy for more information.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in several ways:

Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies using our cookie banner.

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Opting Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • The website will still function normally
  • Your preferences may not be remembered between visits
  • We won't be able to track affiliate referrals (this doesn't affect your purchases)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
