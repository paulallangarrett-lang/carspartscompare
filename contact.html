Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I place an order on your site?

We are a price comparison service, not a retailer. To purchase parts, click through to your preferred retailer (Euro Car Parts, GSF, etc.) and complete your order on their website.

The price shown was different on the retailer's site?

Prices can change frequently and may vary based on your specific vehicle. Always confirm the final price on the retailer's website before purchasing.

How do I know if a part fits my car?

Always enter your vehicle registration on the retailer's website to confirm exact fitment. Part compatibility can vary by engine code, build date, and specification.

Do you offer refunds?

As we don't sell parts, we cannot process refunds. Please contact the retailer where you made your purchase for returns and refunds.
