Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

CarPartsCompare.co.uk ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Vehicle Registration Numbers: When you search for parts, we use your VRM to identify your vehicle. This is cached to improve your experience.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our site, including pages visited, clicks, and search queries.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • IP Address: Used for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate vehicle-specific part comparisons
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyse usage patterns and trends
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie banner or your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and usage statistics
  • Affiliate Networks (Awin): To track purchases made through our links
  • Retailer Websites: When you click through to a retailer, their privacy policy applies

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary:

  • Vehicle lookups: Cached for up to 30 days to improve performance
  • Analytics data: Retained according to Google Analytics policies
  • Click tracking: Retained for 12 months for reporting purposes

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@carspartscompare.co.uk

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@carspartscompare.co.uk
